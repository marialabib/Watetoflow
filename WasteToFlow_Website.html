The Problem

A Triple Crisis.
One Untapped Solution.

Egypt faces simultaneous crises in energy, waste management, and data infrastructure. They are not separate problems — they are one system waiting to be unlocked.

⚡

Energy

Daily power cuts up to 3 hours in Cairo (2023/2024). Volatile fossil fuel prices. No decentralized renewable alternative at scale.

♻️

Waste

25,000 tons of municipal waste daily in Cairo. 58–75% organic. No energy recovery. No systematic tracking. No data infrastructure.

📊

Data

No ESG reporting system for waste operations. Fragmented informal sector. No digital infrastructure. No investment basis for impact capital.

Business Model

Six Revenue Streams.
One Organic Input.

Diversified, resilient and scalable revenue from day one. Every ton of organic waste unlocks multiple value streams simultaneously.

01

Tipping Fees

Hotels, schools & factories pay per ton of organic waste delivered to the facility.

02

Electricity Sales

Direct industrial offtake via private lines or regulated feed-in tariff (PM Decree 4096/2025).

03

Bio-fertilizer

Digestate sold to Egyptian agriculture. Nutrient return and circular economy in action.

04

Carbon Credits

Certified methane avoidance credits via Verra (VCS) or Gold Standard. Tradeable on voluntary carbon markets.

05

ESG Reporting Fees

Verified impact data packages for corporate clients needing CSRD-compliant sustainability reporting.

06

ESG SaaS

Scalable platform — second city, third city. Recurring revenue from real-time ESG infrastructure.
